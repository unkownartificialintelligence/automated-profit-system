### When to Discount:

**DO discount for:**
- First-time customers
- Email list building
- Flash sales (24-48 hours)
- Holidays
- Abandoned carts

**DON'T discount for:**
- Regular sales (devalues brand)
- Every customer
- Long periods (creates expectation)

**Sweet spot:** 10-15% off for most promotions
