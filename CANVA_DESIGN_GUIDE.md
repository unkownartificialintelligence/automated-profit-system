## Step-by-Step Canva Instructions:

### 1. Open Canva Template (2 minutes)
1. Go to **Canva.com**
2. Search: **"t-shirt design"** or **"custom t-shirt"**
3. Select any template (or create custom size: 4500 x 5400 pixels)

### 2. Design Your Cat Dad Shirt (10 minutes)

**Option A - Text-Based Design (Easiest, 5 min):**
```
Main Text: "CAT DAD"
Font: Bold, sans-serif (like Montserrat Bold or Bebas Neue)
Size: Large, centered
Color: Black or Navy Blue

Optional subtitle: "The Man. The Myth. The Legend."
```

**Option B - Graphic + Text (More Engaging, 10 min):**
```
Top: Cat paw prints or silhouette
Center: "CAT DAD" in bold letters
Bottom: "Est. [Year]" or funny tagline
Colors: Black, gray, or navy on white/light gray shirt
```

**Option C - Vintage Style (Premium Look, 15 min):**
```
Retro badge/circle design
"CAT DAD" across center
"Official Member" at top
"Since [Year]" at bottom
Distressed/vintage texture overlay
```

### 3. Canva Elements to Use (Search in Canva):

**Search Terms:**
- "cat silhouette"
- "paw print"
- "vintage badge"
- "retro circle frame"
- "dad badge"

**Recommended Fonts:**
- Montserrat (modern, clean)
- Bebas Neue (bold, impactful)
- Oswald (strong, masculine)
- Anton (thick, attention-grabbing)

**Color Schemes:**
- **Classic:** Black text on white shirt
- **Bold:** Navy blue on heather gray
- **Playful:** Orange/brown cat colors on cream
- **Modern:** Charcoal gray on light blue

## After You Create the Design:

### Upload to Printful (10 minutes):

1. Go to: **printful.com/dashboard/default/products**
2. Click: **"Add Product"**
3. Select: **"Unisex Staple T-Shirt"** (Bella+Canvas 3001)
4. Click: **"Start designing"**
5. Upload your PNG file
6. Position design on front of shirt (centered, about 4" below collar)
7. Click: **"Proceed to Mockups"**
8. Generate mockups (shows how it looks)
9. Click: **"Proceed to Product Details"**
10. Set pricing:
    - **Your retail price:** $24.99
